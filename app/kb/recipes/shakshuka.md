## Shakshuka

Eggs poached in a spiced tomato and pepper sauce. Perfect for breakfast or dinner.

### Steps
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
2. Add diced bell pepper and garlic, cook 2 more minutes.
3. Stir in paprika and cumin, cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
4. Add crushed tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Simmer 10 minutes until thickened.
5. Make small wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them.
6. Cover and cook 5-8 minutes until egg whites are set but yolks are still runny.
7. Serve with crusty bread for dipping.
