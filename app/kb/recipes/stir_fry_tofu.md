## Tofu Stir Fry

A quick and colourful weeknight stir fry.

### Steps
1. Press tofu for 15 minutes, then cut into cubes. Toss with cornstarch.
2. Heat sesame oil in a wok over high heat. Fry tofu until golden on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove.
3. Add a splash more oil. Stir fry broccoli, sliced bell pepper, and carrot for 3 minutes.
4. Add minced garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds.
5. Return tofu to the wok. Add soy sauce and toss everything together.
6. Serve over steamed rice.
