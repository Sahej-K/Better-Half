## Chickpea Curry

A hearty, creamy vegan curry served over rice.

### Steps
1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saute diced onion for 5 minutes.
2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cook 1 minute.
3. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, and cumin. Toast spices 30 seconds.
4. Add diced tomatoes and coconut milk. Stir well.
5. Add drained chickpeas. Bring to a simmer and cook 15 minutes.
6. Season with salt to taste.
7. Serve over steamed rice with fresh cilantro if desired.
