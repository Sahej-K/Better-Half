## French Omelette

A classic, silky French omelette in under 5 minutes.

### Steps
1. Crack 3 eggs into a bowl and whisk vigorously with a fork. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Heat butter in a nonstick pan over medium-high heat until foaming.
3. Pour in eggs and stir quickly with a spatula, shaking the pan.
4. When mostly set but still slightly wet on top, sprinkle cheese and chives on one half.
5. Fold the omelette in half and slide onto a plate.
6. Serve immediately.
