## Roasted Vegetables

Simple, delicious oven-roasted vegetables as a side or main.

### Steps
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Cut sweet potato, zucchini, bell pepper, and red onion into bite-sized pieces.
3. Toss vegetables with olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
4. Spread in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
5. Roast 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway, until edges are golden and caramelised.
6. Serve as a side or over grains.
